Find the Treasure

:::: How to play? ::::

  • A treasure is placed in one of the boxes from the 4x4 boxes
  • Your goal is to find the correct box out of the 16 boex within the minimum time,you can do so by clicking on the boxes
  • If you pick the right/correct box i.e. if you find the treasure it will turn green and you win or else if the treasure is not present there then the box will turn red
